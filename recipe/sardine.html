Rich Sardine & Cabbage Tomato Soup

20 min

Time

4 days

Fridge Storage

1 month

Freezer Storage

Categories: Weight-loss, Fertility-friendly, Easy Dinner

Ingredients

Servings: 2

  • ◎ Sardines (1 can)
  • ◎ Cabbage (3–4 leaves)
  • ◎ Tomatoes (1–2)
  • Onion (optional)
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Water 400ml
  • Milk or cream 50ml
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. ◎ Sauté garlic and onion.
  2. ◎ Add cabbage and tomatoes.
  3. Pour water and simmer.
  4. Add sardines and milk.
  5. Adjust seasoning.

Allergy Information

Contains fish and dairy.

Tips

Great for meal prep. Reheat gently.
